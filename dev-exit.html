The Squeeze

Three pressures every developer at PC knows.

01

Rate pain

Your development loan is running at 10% or more. We can refinance you to roughly 8% all-in — and stop the meter from running on every unsold unit.

02

The extension trap

Your lender wants 2% to extend, again. We give you a clean 12-month term — no extension fees, no quarterly negotiation, no quiet pressure to drop your asking price.

03

Opportunity cost

Capital tied up in finished stock means sites missed at auction. We release working capital alongside the refinance, so you can move on the next deal as a cash buyer.

Qualifying Criteria

Built for serious developers.

We don’t do first-time developers, partly built schemes, or speculative planning plays. Our panel funds finished or near-finished stock — fast, and at scale.

  • Scheme is at Practical Completion or 95% complete
  • You have 2 or more completed schemes behind you
  • Your development loan is expiring — or you want to exit early
  • You want to sell at your pace, not the bank’s
  • Loan size between £500,000 and £10,000,000
  • Site located in England, Scotland or Wales
Lender Panel
