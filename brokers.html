Regulatory Firewall

What You Do. What You Don't.

Posfin operates under the FCA Mere Introducer framework. Your role is strictly limited — which protects you from compliance risk and keeps everything clean.

✓ You Do This
01

Pass the Introduction

Name, contact number, and loan requirement. Three pieces of information. That's the full extent of your role as an introducer.

✗ You Never Do This
02

No Advice. No Fact-Finds.

Do not advise on loan products, assess suitability, or complete fact-finds — especially for regulated mortgage contracts. This is the FCA regulatory firewall that protects you.

✓ We Handle This
03

Everything Else

Qualification, AVM, underwriting, lender selection, documentation, KYC, solicitors, completion, and investor reporting. You introduce — we execute.
