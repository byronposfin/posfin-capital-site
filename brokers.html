The Rules

Clean. Simple. Protected.

This is a Mere Introducer arrangement under FCA guidelines. The rules exist to protect you from compliance risk — and to keep the model clean for everyone.

✓ You Do This

Your Role — Start to Finish

  • →Register the client's name, number, and loan requirement via the portal
  • →Upload supporting documents on behalf of your client — or direct them to do it themselves
  • →Check the portal for status updates
  • →Invoice Posfin Capital on confirmation of drawdown
  • →That's it. Seriously.
✗ You Never Do This

What Posfin Owns

  • ✕Contact or negotiate with any lender on any case
  • ✕Advise the client on which product or lender to use
  • ✕Complete fact-finds or assess suitability — especially regulated mortgages
  • ✕Call Posfin for deal status updates — the portal shows everything
  • ✕Discuss lender terms, rates, or conditions with the borrower
