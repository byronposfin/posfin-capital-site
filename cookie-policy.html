What Are Cookies?

A cookie is a small text file placed on your device when you visit a website. Cookies allow websites to remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your experience on future visits.

Cookies are harmless — they cannot damage your device, access other files, or identify you as an individual. They simply store information about your visit so that the site works better for you.

Posfin Capital uses cookies to ensure our website functions correctly, to analyse how it is used, and to make our marketing more relevant. We will never use cookies to collect personal information without your knowledge or consent.

Help limit how often you see an ad and measure campaign effectiveness. Meta Pixel — retargeting on Facebook and Instagram. Google Ads — remarketing audiences. Up to 90 days

3

Why We Use Cookies

Cookies help us provide you with the best possible experience on our website. Specifically, we use them to:

  • Ensure the website and enquiry forms work correctly
  • Understand how visitors navigate our site so we can improve it
  • Measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns (Facebook Ads, Google Ads)
  • Show you relevant adverts when you visit other websites — only if you have consented
  • Remember your preferences so you are not asked the same questions repeatedly
  • Protect the security and integrity of our website
4

Your Consent & Control

Under the UK Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) and UK GDPR, we are required to obtain your consent before placing non-essential cookies on your device.

Cookie Preferences

When you first visit posfincapital.com, you will be presented with a cookie consent notice. You can choose to accept all cookies, accept essential cookies only, or customise your preferences. You can change your preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

Strictly necessary cookies do not require your consent as they are essential for the website to function. All other cookie categories require your explicit consent before being activated.

Withdrawing consent will not affect the lawfulness of any processing already carried out. However, some website features may not function as intended without non-essential cookies.
