The Process

From enquiry to drawdown in two weeks

Structured, fast, professional. No unnecessary delays built in.

01
Indicative terms — same day
Submit your enquiry. We assess the deal, run an automated valuation, and structure initial terms. You'll have indicative figures before the end of the day.
02
Formal application — 24–48 hours
Once you accept indicative terms, we package the formal application to your matched lender. Document requirements are straightforward and we guide you through exactly what's needed.
03
Lender offer — Week 1
The lender issues a formal offer, typically within 5–7 working days of receiving the complete application. We review all terms before presenting to you.
04
Legal & valuation — Week 1–2
We work with Ackroyd Legal to handle solicitor requirements. An independent valuation is instructed where required. Both processes run simultaneously to minimise elapsed time.
05
Drawdown — Day 10–15
Once legal and valuation are complete, funds are drawn down and transferred. In most cases the entire process from enquiry to completion runs 10–15 working days.
