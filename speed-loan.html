Why Speed Matters

The cost of waiting is higher than you think

Speed Loans exist for one reason: when the deal can't wait for the normal process.

01
Your bank won't move fast enough
High street banks take 6–12 weeks minimum. Bridging decisions at Posfin happen same day. The window closes before your bank has even assigned a case manager.
02
Solicitors are slowing things down
Standard bridging requires solicitors on both sides. Speed Loans use an equitable charge — no solicitor instruction required. That eliminates weeks of back-and-forth.
03
You need certainty, not a timeline
Auctions, chain breaks, vendor ultimatums. When someone says "funds or we walk," you need to be able to say yes in 72 hours. That's exactly what a Speed Loan delivers.
Who Qualifies

Designed for straightforward deals at sensible LTV

Speed Loans are high-certainty, low-complexity products. If you tick these boxes, we can move immediately.

  • Residential or commercial property in England & Wales
  • Loan amount between £26K and £500K
  • Combined debt (including this loan) under 50% net LTV
  • First or second charge considered
  • Clear, credible exit strategy in place
  • No requirement for borrower's own solicitor
  • No upfront valuation or legal costs
How It Works

Four steps from enquiry to funded

No committees, no committees, no delays. Just a clear process designed around your urgency.

01
60-second form
Tell us the property, the loan amount, and your exit. That's all we need to start. No lengthy applications, no document packs at this stage.
02
Same-day decision
We run an automated valuation on the property and assess your LTV position. You'll have a clear yes or no — and indicative terms — within hours of your enquiry.
03
Terms issued within 24 hours
If you accept, formal terms follow within 24 hours. We use an equitable charge structure, which means no solicitor instruction on the borrower side. Review and sign.
04
Funds in 72 hours
Once terms are signed and the charge is registered, funds are released. In most cases that's 72 hours from the point of instruction. No surprises.
