Who Qualifies

Fast funding for homeowners who need to move.

We lend against your property. No credit checks. No proof of income. No upfront costs. If the equity is there, we can move.

  • Residential or commercial property in England or Wales
  • Net loan amount between £26,000 and £500,000
  • Combined debt (existing mortgage + new loan) under 50% of property value
  • First or second charge — we work alongside your existing mortgage
  • Property owner — freehold or leasehold
  • Funds needed within 1–10 working days
  • Clear exit strategy — sale, refinance, or business income
Not for us
  • Combined debt above 50% LTV
  • Loans below £26,000 or above £500,000
  • Properties in Scotland or Northern Ireland (second charge only)

Speed loans are only offered once 12-month main loan terms are also in place — this gives speed loan lenders comfort that a clear exit exists.
