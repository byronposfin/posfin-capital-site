All UK lenders require a clean 2nd charge position. This means any existing subordinate charges (2nd, 3rd, 4th etc.), arrears, charging orders, and undertakings must be redeemed in full from the new loan proceeds at completion. Our solicitors will handle the redemption process — the costs come from the loan, not from you — but you should factor these into the amount you need to borrow.

Zero upfront costs on Speed Loans

All fees, interest, and charges are deducted from the loan at drawdown — nothing to pay out of pocket. Unlike a Main Loan which typically requires a £500 broker advance, solicitor costs, and valuation fees before completion.

Rollover available at term end

Need more time? Speed Loans can be rolled over for an additional 3 or 6 months at the end of your term, subject to equity remaining within 50% LTV. No re-application — just an extension.

Graduate to a Main Loan

Borrowing under £100k? Start with a Speed Loan, then transition to a brokered Main Loan facility when you're ready for a longer-term solution. We handle the entire refinance — you're already on our book.

6 months
