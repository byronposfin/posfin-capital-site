Why Speed Matters

Three reasons a normal bridging loan won't work right now.

01

The bank won't move fast enough

You need cash in days, not weeks. High-street lenders take 4–8 weeks minimum. We fund in 3–5 days — because we use specialist lenders who are built for exactly this.

02

Solicitors are slowing everything down

Most bridging loans require borrower solicitors, survey reports and legal packs. We do not. Equitable charge means we can move before any of that is in place.

03

You need certainty, not a maybe

Auction deadlines, tax bills, urgent repairs, a deal that won't wait. When the clock is running, the answer has to be yes or no — same day. That's what we give you.

Who Qualifies

Fast funding for homeowners who need to move.

We lend against your property. No credit checks. No proof of income. No upfront costs. If the equity is there, we can move.

  • Residential or commercial property in England or Wales
  • Net loan amount between £50,000 and £300,000
  • Combined debt (existing mortgage + new loan) under 50% of property value
  • First or second charge — we work alongside your existing mortgage
  • Property owner — freehold or leasehold
  • Funds needed within 1–10 working days
  • Clear exit strategy — sale, refinance, or business income
