Eligibility

Do you qualify?

We are selective. Post-shipment finance only. No exceptions.

✓ We Can Help
  • Goods shipped with Bill of Lading
  • Confirmed irrevocable LC or SBLC
  • Registered company 2+ years
  • Minimum £300,000 transaction
  • Filed accounts 2 years minimum
  • Buyers in non-sanctioned countries
  • Clear source of funds
  • Insurable buyer portfolio
✗ We Cannot Help
  • Pre-shipment finance
  • Companies under 12 months
  • Buyer/seller same group
  • Sanctioned countries (Iran, Syria, North Korea, Lebanon)
  • No verifiable buyer contracts
  • Transactions below £300,000
  • Commodities with mismatched pricing
  • Advance fee fraud or phantom shipments
