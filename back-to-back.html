How It Works

Two stages. One clean outcome.

Back-to-Back Bridge combines the speed of a Speed Loan with the leverage of a permanent product. The Speed Loan redeems automatically at drawdown of the permanent facility.

Stage 1
Speed Loan
Day 1–3
  • Up to 50% net LTV — clean, defensible position
  • £26K–£500K funded in 72 hours
  • Equitable charge — no borrower solicitors required
  • Immediate liquidity to secure or act on the opportunity
  • Min. 1 month or 50% of term · subject to lender
Stage 2
Permanent Solution
Concurrent — drawn down when ready
  • Main Bridge — up to 70% LTV, 10–15 days
  • Development Finance — up to 65% GDV, ~30 days
  • Development Exit — up to 75% GDV, ~15 days
  • Speed Loan redeems automatically at drawdown
  • No double-funding gap, no exposure window
Common Pairings

Match your situation to the right structure

Back-to-Back Bridge works across a wide range of scenarios. These are the most common pairings.

Speed + Main Bridge
Chain break or urgent purchase
Speed Loan secures the property in 72 hours. Main Bridge at up to 70% LTV follows within 10–15 days. Speed Loan redeems at drawdown.
Speed + Dev Finance
Development site acquisition
Speed Loan acquires the site immediately. Development Finance facility follows — up to 65% GDV with day-one and staged drawdowns. No gap between acquisition and build start.
Speed + Dev Exit
Scheme complete, dev loan expiring
Speed Loan bridges the gap while Dev Exit at up to 75% GDV is structured. Removes time pressure from scheme sales.
Speed + Auction Finance
28-day completion requirement
Auction purchase requires completion in 28 days. Speed Loan funds in 72 hours, well within the deadline. Main Bridge or mortgage follows at leisure.
