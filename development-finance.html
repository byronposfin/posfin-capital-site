The bank problem

Your high-street bank wants 6 to 12 weeks to give you an answer. The vendor will not wait. We give you indicative terms the same day — and funds in 30 working days.

Planning permission has an expiry date. Every week chasing funding is a week lost from your build programme. We move at developer speed because we are funded by lenders who do.

What We Fund

Built for developers who build.

We fund experienced developers with sites, full planning, and a clear exit. We do not fund speculative land plays or first-time builders — and we will tell you straight if your scheme is not a fit.

  • Ground-up new build (houses, flats, mixed-use)

High Leverage Option

Up to 85% of costs.
Different product. Different basis.

Where the numbers work on a cost basis rather than GDV, specialist lenders will fund up to 85% of total project costs — day-one land draw plus 100% of build costs drawn in stages.

This is not the same product as our standard 65% LTGDV facility. It suits developers with strong schemes, strong margin, and limited equity to deploy upfront.

Important: LTC-based lending is assessed on build cost and margin — not end value. GDV remains the exit, but the loan quantum is tied to what you spend, not what you sell for. Tell us your full cost stack.
