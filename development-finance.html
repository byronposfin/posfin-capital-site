The Process

From appraisal to first drawdown in 30 days

Development Finance involves more structure than bridging, but we run every stage concurrently to minimise elapsed time.

01
Initial appraisal — same day
Submit your scheme summary, GDV estimate, and build cost. We appraise the deal and confirm whether it fits the panel's appetite before you invest time in a full pack.
02
Indicative terms — 24–48 hours
If the appraisal stacks, we issue indicative terms covering facility size, day-one advance, drawdown schedule, rate, and exit strategy requirements.
03
Formal application & GDV report — Week 1–2
Lender instructs an independent GDV / QS report. We package the formal application concurrently. You prepare the planning documents and build contract.
04
Legal — Week 2–3
Solicitors instructed on both sides. Charge registered. Drawdown schedule agreed and set into the facility agreement.
05
First drawdown — Day 25–30
Day-one land advance drawn. Build drawdowns follow as each certified stage of construction is completed and inspected by the monitoring surveyor.
