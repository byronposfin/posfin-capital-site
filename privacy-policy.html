The Type of Personal Information We Collect

We currently collect and process the following information:

  • Personal identifiers, contacts, and characteristics (e.g., name and contact details).
  • For loan applications, this may include home and security addresses, property values, outstanding debt values, title charges, and related information.
3

How We Get Personal Information and Why We Have It

Most personal information is provided to us directly by you. This is done to help qualify, assess, and provide you with a finance offer from an unsecured or secured lender.

We also receive personal information indirectly from the following sources:

  • Publicly available online sites such as Zoopla, Rightmove, and other property valuation websites.
  • HMLR Land Registry.
Usage: We use the information you provide to assess and present your case to our network of private and mainstream lenders to support your funding inquiry. We may share this information with these lenders as part of the process to generate a finance offer and appropriate terms.

Lawful Bases: Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), our lawful bases for processing this information are:

  • We have a contractual obligation.
  • We have a legal obligation.
4

How We Store Your Personal Information

Your information is stored securely on our systems and associated CRM platforms.

We keep this information for up to six years. After this period, we dispose of your information through manual and automated removal on an annual basis.

If you wish to re-engage our services after this time, you will need to submit a new inquiry with new consent.

5

Your Data Protection Rights

Under data protection law, you have the following rights:

Right of Access

You have the right to ask for copies of your personal information.

Right to Rectification

You have the right to ask us to correct information you believe is inaccurate or incomplete.

Right to Erasure

You have the right to ask us to erase your personal information in certain circumstances.

Right to Restriction

You have the right to ask us to restrict the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances.

Right to Object

You have the right to object to the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances.

Right to Data Portability

You have the right to ask that we transfer the information you gave us to another organisation, or to you, in certain circumstances.

You are not required to pay a fee to exercise these rights. We have one month to respond to your request. To make a request, please contact us using the details provided above.
6

How to Complain

If you have concerns about our use of your personal information, you can contact us directly at the details provided above.

You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you are unhappy with how we have handled your data.
