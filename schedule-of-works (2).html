Schedule of Works

To finalise your facility, we require a detailed schedule of the works planned at the security property. This document supports lender underwriting, drawdown sequencing, and surveyor instruction. All figures are treated in strict confidence.

We've pre-filled some details from your file. Please review each section and update if anything has changed.
SECTION 01

Borrower

Your contact details so we can match this submission to your file.

SECTION 02

Security Property

The property at which the works will be carried out.

SECTION 03

Project Overview

High-level summary of the works and the resulting end value.

SECTION 04

Itemised Schedule

Break the project into work packages or phases. Add as many as required. Costs should reflect tendered quotes where available; clearly mark estimates as such in the description.

SECTION 05

Lead Contractor

The primary contractor or project manager carrying out the works. Sub-contractors can be listed in the notes.

SECTION 06

Planning & Compliance

Regulatory and consent position of the project. Be straightforward — lenders prefer disclosed risk to discovered risk.

SECTION 07

Funding Breakdown

How the works are being funded. The sum of these figures should reconcile to the total works budget in Section 03.

SECTION 08

Risk & Exit

Contingency, identified risks and your route to repaying the bridge.

Lenders typically expect 10–15% contingency on heavy refurb and above.
SECTION 09

Supporting Documents

Provide a shared link (Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive) to your supporting documents. Ensure the link is set to "anyone with the link can view".

SECTION 10

Declaration & Signature

The figures and statements above will be relied upon by Posfin Capital and the funding lender(s). Please sign below to confirm accuracy.
